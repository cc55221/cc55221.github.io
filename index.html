About

I am a researcher working on UAV-assisted wireless networks and immersive 360° video streaming, with a focus on reinforcement learning (PPO), graph neural networks for dynamic topology encoding, and NOMA/RIS-based transmission optimization. I am broadly interested in how multi-UAV scheduling and semantic-aware streaming can improve QoE and spectrum efficiency in dynamic environments.
